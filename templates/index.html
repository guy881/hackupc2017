What does My Local Bartender do?

My Local Bartender is a platform that will connect cocktail mixologists, bartenders and general bar staff with people looking to hire them for work or private events.

How does it work?

Customers will be able to put up ads for events and search bartenders, bartenders will be able to offer their services privately to a large range of customers.

How much does it cost to join MLB?

It's Free to join as both a bar professional or a customer and there are no subscription fees. MLB will take a small fee when a bar professional is hired for work or an event.

What kind of events can bartenders work at?

Anywhere where alcohol's appropriate (or at least present). Parties, weddings, private gatherings, corporate functions, even cover for bar staff at venues, bars, restaurants etc.

How does MLB work for Bartenders and Mixologists?

Sign up free to My Local Bartender and create a profile detailing your skills and experience. Your full profile will only be visible to customers signed into the site looking to hire a bar professional. You'll be able to search ads and offer your services to customers at a rate of your choosing.

How does MLB work for people looking for bartenders?

Sign up free to My Local Bartender and create a simple profile. You can then create adverts for the events you need bartenders for and be able search through our bar professional database. You'll be able to contact bartenders through MLB for your work or events.

How will it logistically work?

When a customer and bartender agree on an event, the rate the customer pays will be calculated using the bartenders hourly rate, the estimate duration of the event and MLB's fee; a small percentage added onto the total. The customer will pay the amount before the event, which is released to the bartender after the event has taken place.

I'm a bar professional, will I have to bring my own kit?

The customer will specify beforehand what you'll need to bring with you. If they require you to supply mixing equipment, glasses, alcohol and anything else needed for the job, you'll be able to factor these costs into your rate for the job, which the customer will be informed beforehand of.

Whats My Local Bartenders story?

MLB is envisaged by Rajeet Singh. Rajeet started mixing cocktails as a hobby which quickly turned into an obsession. He was mixing any chance he got; at parties, dinners, soon he was offering his mixing services to friends and others, but he couldn't find anywhere online to connect to more people for cocktail based events, and so My Local Bartender was born.
